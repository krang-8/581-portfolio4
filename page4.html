CPSC 581: Project 3 (Phone Down Game)

Overall Description

Group: Dheeraj Kumar, Irtaza Ansari, Mahmoud Abdelaty, and Leslie Nguyen

Technology has aided in advancing human connects in more meaningful ways by giving society different channels to connect. Social media has transformed videos and image sharing as a meaning of staying close to one another. However, it has also created more distance in many relationships. Things posed on social media may not represent the whole truth and people are growing addicted to their devices uninterested in real connections. No one wants to talk to someone who is barely paying attention to them on their phone.

That is why our group created a digital platform to keep you off your phone! Our platform is meant to be used in a group setting to improve communication, conversation, collaboration. All people in the group turn on platform and enter in your information. Then they place their phones face down and when the phone is picked up it will showcase a red screen indicating it has been handled. The platform prevents the user from getting distracted and picking up their phone out of habit so that they can pay attention to real life communication and be able to have meaningful conversations with the ones that truly matter. Thus, allowing important collaboration. 

Initial Sketches

WatchBlink

A new way of seamlessly connecting and communicating with loved ones that live far away, simply pair two smart watches together and once you tap on one, the other "blinks" or lights up.


AR History

A VR tool used to enhance history lessons, in this example the teacher and students are present outside the pyramids which can make the teacher's job easier explaining certain historical sites.


Memory Unlock

An AR tool used to enhance collaboration and communication, in this example the users are brought into a semi virtual campfire where they can gather and chat. 

VR Chat!

A VR tool allowing you to see other people in a call in real-time through a VR headset.

Homie Tap!

Tap your phone to chat to ping a random nearby chatter and start a conversation!


NFC Business Card

This idea is used to enhance communication and advertising, using NFC technology in your phone to make your phone act as a portable "Smart Business Card".

Score Counter

This idea is simple, a score counter that can stay in sync between 2 phones so users can focus on communicating during their minigames and let the phone act as just a scorekeeper to avoid distractions!

Meme Tap!

Tap your phone, if there is another user nearby your phone vibrates and the other user receives a random meme to lighten up their day! Enhancing communication by allowing people to reach out to others and put a smile on their face without even knowing them. This idea was inspired by the fact that a lot of people are "shy". 

NO PICKUP!

A minigame used to enhance communication and collaboration between folks. The premise is very simple, you put your phones down and the first person to lift up their phone loses. We would use the phone's gyroscope to measure movement and find the loser.

Quit Tap

A minigame used to enhance communication and collaboration between folks. This idea was an "extreme" version on the previous idea. Instead of using the gyroscope, this time forcing the users to not even touch their phone instead!

Write-up for detailed sketches :

The sketches combine ideas from all group members. Each detailed sketch surrounds the idea of communication through utilizing different modes of interaction with users. Some utilize text-based exchange and other only use images or application contact to help users connect with each other.
The group ended up choosing the idea of a digital platform that keeps people off their devices to strength real life conversations (sketch 5). The platform is a novel way to keep people attentive to each other and motivated to engage in building meaningful relationships with each other.

Overall, I contributed by helping in ideation, creating my sketches and also filming and directing the demo and storyboard along with the help of my team.
