CPSC 581: Project 2 (MEMORY UNLOCK &
SECRET HANDSHAKE UNLOCK)

Overall Description

Everyone carries a phone in today’s world. Our phones hold with them information about us that we need. In many cases our phones know us better than we know ourselves. Therefore, it is important to keep our devices secured so that we are the only ones who can unlock them.

Traditional methods of unlocking phones with passwords and bio detectors like face and finger scans have kept us safe. However, these methods of security could use a creative update!

Our group wants to introduce two new ways to unlock our phones. The first unlocking technique is based on gesture input: as patterns light up buttons the user must watch for their secret pattern to be displayed and then repeat that pattern correctly to unlock. The second unlock technique is based on phone sensor data: as the user must interact with the phone’s camera as another person’s hand and act out their secret handshake correctly to unlock.

Initial Sketches - Touch Based Unlock

Pattern Unlock

A simple unlocking mechanism built around completing a pattern by clicking on a bubble sequentially out of 9 bubbles.

This idea was chosen as we felt it is a simple enough idea that we are able to implement it with ease while also being able to build and improve on it to make it more novel.

Memory Unlock

This idea is built around unlocking your phone through a memory game.

This idea was chosen as we felt it is a simple enough idea that we are able to implement it with ease while also being able to build and improve on it by combining it with another idea to make it more novel.

Math Unlock

A touch unlock mechanism built on the idea of answering a simple math question as sort of a verification that you are an adult. You simply tap on the correct answer to unlock your device.

This idea was not chosen due to it lacking security and lacking the ability to expand on the idea to make it more novel.

Favourite Gallery Unlock

This idea is simple, you are greeted with 3 images from your gallery, you must pick 1 out of the 3 which you have saved in your favourites. You simply tap on the image which is favourited to unlock your device

This idea was not chosen due to the fact that it is such a specific use case and requires the user to set it up by having a lot of images in their gallery and going through the process of favouriting a lot of images.

Translation Unlock

Teach yourself a new language by incorporating it into your daily life through unlocking your phone. You simply fill in the blanks for the translation by clicking on the letter bubbles to unlock your device.

This idea was not chosen due to the high chance of getting locked out of your device.

Osu! Unlock

Play a minigame while unlocking your phone! Track the bubble and move it to the next target successfully to unlock your device. This idea was inspired by the video game Osu!

This idea was not chosen due to it's lack of malleability to making it novel.

Guess The Flag

Expand your general knowledge and geographical skills by incorporating it into your daily life through unlocking your phone. You simply select the answer option choice with the country name that corresponds to the flag to unlock your phone.

This idea was not chosen due to lack of interest from group members.

Trivia Unlock

In this idea, you get prompted with a general knowledge question, answer it correctly to unlock your device.

This idea was not chosen due to lack of interest from the team.

Higher or Lower!

A minigame where you are shown images of two different, unique objects and you have to guess which has a higher/lower average price. By completing the minigame, you unlock your phone!

We were not able to think of many ways to make it unique as the video game "Higher or Lower" already exists with the same purpose and features. Hence, it was eliminated. Also, it seemed like a random thing to rely on to consistently unlock your device.

Guess The Song

A minigame where you are prompted with song lyrics with the goal of being able to correctly guess the name of the song. By clicking on the bubble with the right cover art of the song, you unlock your phone.

This idea was not chosen as we felt it required a lot of time to gather information about song and lyrics.

Initial Sketches - Sensor Based Unlock

Wifi Sensor

This idea is built around using WiFi signal to unlock your phone, it can be used for children to ensure they can only access the internet inside the house where they can be under adult supervision.

This idea was not chosen due to difficulty of implementation and lack of time.

Bluetooth Sensor

This idea is built around using Bluetooth signal to unlock your phone, it can be used for children to ensure they can only access the internet when nearby trusted devices where they are under adult supervision.

This idea was chosen due to it's ability of being malleable enough to make it novel and special, we were able to think of a variety of detailed ideas on how to make it unique and interactive while keeping it's simplicity.

Fingerprint Sensor

Simple idea, unlock your phone through a fingerprint sensor located somewhere on the device's chassis.

This idea was not chosen due to lack of novelty.

Temperature Sensor

This idea is built around the concept of unlocking your phone only under certain weather conditions, this idea was inspired by me going on hikes as I wish to use my phone less when interacting with nature, usually on hikes at higher altitudes the temperatures are lower which can be used to lock the phone temporarily.

This idea was not chosen as it could provide problems using it consistently and could lock you out in sudden temperature drops if you do not fix the configuration.

GPS Sensor

This idea is built around using GPS Location Services to unlock your phone, it can be used for children to ensure they can only access the internet inside the house where they can be under adult supervision.

This idea was not chosen due to difficulty of implementation and lack of time.

Voice Recognition

Unlock your phone through voice recognition by saying a certain phrase.

This idea was not chosen due to lack of interest from team members.

Pose!

Unlock your phone through posing in a certain way infront of your camera. The camera will be used in tandem with a machine learning algorithm to detect poses to unlock your device.

This idea was chosen but will be modified to be more novel and consistently usable.

Car Obstacle

Using sensors commonly present in phones, complete a car obstacle by rotating your phone to move the steering wheel, once the obstacle is completed your phone is unlocked. 

This idea was not chosen as while it does have a useful use case scenario, it is not consistently viable to be used as an unlocking mechanism.

Master Lock

Unlock your phone by tilting it and solving the master lock by rotating it to complete the right combination.

This idea was not chosen due to difficulty of implementation and lack of time.

Angle Unlock

Unlock your phone by tilting it and keeping it in the "secret" angle.

This idea was not chosen due to it being too simple and unsecure.
